ABOUT ME

I am Full Stack Web Developer and entrepreneur. Over the last decade I have served in numerous Communication, Computer Networking and Software Development roles within the military and civilian sector. My Communication career began in the military when I served as the Senior Communication's Sergeant for my Operational Detachment Alpha (ODA) in the Continental United States and countries abroad. After 11 years of service, I retired from active service in 2016 receiving an honorable discharge; I moved to the civilian sector and started working for Level 3 communications which was then bought out by Centurylink in 2018. My roles in the company were Field Technician and One Call Specialist 3. After 3 years of working in the telecommunications industry I decided to leave and focus on my real estate portfolio full time. In 2021, I decided I wanted to take up a new career path and become a full-time software developer. Software Development has always been an interest of mine; I took the Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp through CNM Ingenuity graduating in December 2021.
