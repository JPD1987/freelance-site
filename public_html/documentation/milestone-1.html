PWP-MILESTONE-ONE

Purpose:

This project will be to market my professional portfolio to display my skills learned from the deep dive bootcamp on front-end development.

Audience:

This site is being designed with startup founders, hiring managers, technical recruiters, and peers who are in search of new talent.

Goal:

The goal for this website is to initiate contact with myself in regard to employment, contract work and collaboration of projects through the available contact form and social media websites listed.

Persona

Name: Bill Rollins

Age: 45

Profession: Technical Recruiter with Google, a long-standing Fortune 500 company that is looking to hire full-time college grads or individuals who have completed a bootcamp with applicable experience. They are the Number One site that is used daily to conduct research. The average age for daily users are 13-50 and is an excellent choice for High School and College students.
Technology: Macbook Pro Tower with 2 LG 27" monitors while in the office. He uses a Macbook Pro 13" with Touch-bar with one 34" LG monitor, and an iPhone 13 Pro with unlimited 5G data.
Attitudes and Behaviors: Bill is a very ornery individual who typically doesn't spend more than 2 minutes on anyone's resume. He is a big believer in zip recruiter along with finding talent through social media websites, i.e. LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.
Frustrations and Needs: Bill needs to hire a Junior Software Developer is proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, BootStrap, Angular, Visual Studio and WebStorm. The individual needs to be a team player, one who works well autonomous and within a team environment. The individual also needs to have a high level of maturity due to the remote work environment. Bill is having a hard time selecting the right person for the job who meets the criteria listed. His supervisor gave him a deadline of three weeks to find a new hire.
Goals: Bill's primary concern is choosing the right candidate. Is he able to choose the right candidate with his 2-minute resume review timeframe? If he does will the candidate have the minimum qualifications to conduct the job properly.
User Story: As a user, will he be able to find the right qualities needed to hire.
